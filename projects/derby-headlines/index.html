Of Kentucky Derby Headlines

From mint juleps and presidential visits to muddy tracks and fighting jockeys, we've seen it all. Starting in 1916, The Courier-Journal began dedicating coverage of the Kentucky Derby to the front page. We searched our archives to highlight the front page from each to show you how the race has evolved over the past 100 years. So come and take a trip down memory lane with us.
